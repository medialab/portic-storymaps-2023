## The PORTIC program

The PORTIC research program (ANR-18-CE38-0010) aims to cross-reference data on navigation and the value of France's foreign trade on the eve of the French Revolution to better understand the commercial dynamics at work. To do this, it offers data querying and visualization interfaces adapted to various audiences and interests.

[More information about PORTIC](https://anr.portic.fr/)
